The Team vs Many is designed to perform aggregate damage calculations, allowing you to visualize how one or more Pokémon from your team interact with a group of targets. It is the ultimate tool for evaluating broad team matchups against the meta.

In this view, you select one or two attackers from your team to face off against a list of multiple targets (usually the opposing team).

  • Offensive Testing: See at a glance if your active Pokémon can 1HKO or 2HKO the entire opposing team.
  • Double Targeting: Add a second attacker to simulate how two of your Pokémon combine their damage against each target.
  • Coverage Analysis: Identify which targets your current offensive setup struggles to break through.
group_add

Combined Damage: You can simulate a joint attack by selecting two Pokémon from your team. Hold Ctrl while clicking the second Pokémon, or use the Combined Damage button in the UI.

Core Features

calculate

Mass Calculation: Every change you make to moves, items, or field conditions is instantly recalculated for every single target in the list, giving you immediate feedback on your tactical adjustments.

  • Dynamic Reordering: Use the Order by Damage button to automatically sort targets by the percentage of damage they take. This brings the most relevant interactions, such as guaranteed KOs, to the top of the list.
  • Add Meta: Use the Add Meta button to instantly load common teams and top threats from the current competitive format, saving you from manual entry.
  • Roll Configuration: Customize your calculations by selecting specific damage rolls. You can toggle between different roll levels (like Max, High, or Low) to see how damage varies across all targets.
  • Quick Interaction: Click on any result in the list to open that specific matchup for fine-tuning or secondary calculation.
