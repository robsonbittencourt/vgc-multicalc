Share your optimized spreads and team compositions with others, or save them for later use in Pokémon Showdown.

Export a Single Pokémon

Export any individual Pokémon from your team or the opponent's list. This generates the standard Showdown text format, including EVs, IVs, Nature, and moves.

Export a Full Team

Get your entire 6-Pokémon team in a single text block. This is ideal for sharing your team on social media or importing it into a team builder.

Export Opponents

You can also export the list of Pokémon you've curated in the multi-calc views, making it easy to share meta clusters or specific threat lists.

Share Your Calcs

The Share your calcs feature is the ultimate way to share your work. It generates a unique URL that encodes your entire current session.

  • Total Preservation: The link includes both teams, all field settings, and even the current calculation results.
  • Instant Preview: Anyone with the link can see exactly what you were analyzing without needing to import anything manually.
