VGC Multi Calc provides flexible ways to bring your Pokémon and teams into the tool, saving you from manual stat entry and ensuring your calculations are based on exact sets.

Supported Formats

link

Pokepaste & Raw Text: You can simply paste a Pokepaste URL or the raw Export text from Pokémon Showdown. The tool automatically detects the format and parses the data.

Import a Single Pokémon

Quickly replace a single Pokémon in your team by using the "Import" button located on its build card. This is perfect for testing minor adjustments or swapping a specific team member.

Import a Full Team

Replace your entire 6-Pokémon roster at once using the team-level import. This instantly populates all slots with their respective moves, items, and EVs.

Import Opponents

In Multi Calc modes (Team vs Many or Many vs Team), you can import as many Pokémon as you want into the "Many" list. This allows you to benchmark your team against an entire meta or a specific target list.
